Find answers to common agriculture questions

What are the best practices for crop rotation?

Effective crop rotation involves:

  • Alternating between different plant families
  • Planning 3-4 year rotation cycles
  • Considering nutrient needs of each crop
  • Including cover crops in rotation
How do I control common garden pests naturally?

Natural pest control methods include:

  • Companion planting
  • Introducing beneficial insects
  • Using organic sprays like neem oil
  • Maintaining healthy soil
What are efficient irrigation methods for small farms?

Efficient irrigation methods include:

  • Drip irrigation systems
  • Sprinkler systems with timers
  • Rainwater harvesting
  • Soil moisture monitoring
How can I improve my soil quality?

Improve soil quality through:

  • Regular soil testing
  • Adding organic matter
  • Using cover crops
  • Proper pH management
